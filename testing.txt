  Cross-cutting features to check
                                 
  - Language: Go to Settings → General → Language, switch to e.g. Spanish, then ask for Paella — the recipe should come
  back in Spanish.                                                                                                      
  - Profile preferences: Settings → Profile → set dietary restriction to "vegan", then ask for a burger — it should give
   a vegan version without dairy or meat.                                                                               
  - Chat history: After getting a recipe, refresh the page and reopen that chat from the sidebar — it should render
  identically.                                                                                                          
  - Follow-up memory: Ask Spaghetti carbonara, then ask What can I substitute for pancetta? — the AI should know you
  mean carbonara.
